Welcome to FINQ Help & Support

FINQ helps simplify your family financial management with transparency and ease. Here you get handy guides to make the most of your experience.

Account Setup & Security

- Maintaining strong passwords and enabling verification helps keep your data safe.
- Forgot password? Use the recovery option on login to securely reset your credentials.

Family & Members Management

- Create or join a family group easily.
- Add members by invite email; roles can be managed by the head of the family.

Building Financial Transparency

- Track income and expenses individually or as a family.
- See shared progress on budgets with clear visualization and alerts.

Chat & Collaboration

- Use the built-in chat to discuss transactions securely with your family.
- Share notes, receipts, and stay informed real-time.

Contact & Support

For any assistance beyond this guide, please contact our support team via support@finqapp.com.
